Brainer App UI/UX Design

Brain Trainer is an educational app designed to enhance aptitude learning through interactive exercises and engaging quizzes. The app focuses on improving users' problem-solving skills and logical thinking, making it ideal for students and professionals. With a user-friendly interface designed using Figma, the app provides an intuitive and efficient learning experience. Brain Trainer empowers users to strengthen their cognitive abilities in a fun and accessible way.
